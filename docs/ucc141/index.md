## Course Overview: UCC141-1 - Virtualization Fundamentals (VM)

**Prerequisites**: None

**Objectives**:
* Understand the concepts of hypervisor-based virtualization.
* Be capable of creating, configuring, and managing virtual machines (VMs).

**Content**:
* Concepts of virtualization (Hypervisors Type 1 & 2).
* Creation and management of VMs with tools like VirtualBox or KVM.
* Network configuration of VMs.
* Snapshots and cloning.

**Assessment**:
* Practical Work
* Final Exam
